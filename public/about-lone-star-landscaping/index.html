About Lone Star Landscaping

Lonestar Landscaping is very proud to be in our 13th year, providing professional landscaping solutions throughout the Dallas/Fort Worth metroplex. We specialize in upscale residential and commercial clients by delivering a professional look, based on our years of experience. Our solutions include new designs, installations and ongoing maintenance programs. Our experienced teams of professionals can help you achieve the perfect landscape for any home or business.

Our most popular program, the Complete Maintenance Program, ensures a healthy, manicured look year round through weekly treatments. This program is based on a 12-month cycle, which is designed to keep a standard of appearance second to none throughout the year.
